About Me

I'm Sai Teja Govind — a product designer specializing in AI platforms, data systems, and geospatial tools. I work at the intersection of complex data and usable interfaces, turning enterprise-grade systems into experiences that people actually want to use.

Currently at I Spatial Techno Solutions, where I lead UX for an AI data intelligence suite serving enterprise clients across spatial, environmental, and operational domains. Previously designed government spatial platforms for 2M+ residents in the UAE.

I care about structure over decoration. My work focuses on information architecture, system thinking, and scalable design patterns — the kind of design that holds up when the product grows from 3 modules to 30.
