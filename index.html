About Me


I'm Sai Teja Govind, a UX/UI designer based in the UAE.

I spend most of my time on products where getting lost has real consequences — mapping platforms, data dashboards, systems where a confused user isn't just frustrated, they're stuck.

My last project was a deployed UAE government geospatial platform serving residents finding hospitals and government administrators managing spatial data — on the same interface. That kind of tension between simplicity and power is exactly where I do my best work.
